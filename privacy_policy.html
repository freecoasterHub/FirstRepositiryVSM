Privacy Policy for MemoCards

Effective Date: July 2, 2024

We respect the privacy of users of our application MemoCards. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding this information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

MemoCards does not collect personal information such as name, address, or contact details. The app uses Firebase anonymous authentication to create a unique user identifier.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When using the app, we may automatically collect certain information such as device type, unique device identifiers, operating system, and other diagnostic information.

2. How We Use Collected Information

2.1 To Improve the Service

The collected information helps us improve the functionality and performance of the app. For example, usage data allows us to better understand how users interact with various features.

2.2 Authentication

We use Firebase for anonymous user authentication to provide access to their data and settings.

3. Permissions

3.1 Access to Local Storage

The app requests permission to access your device's local storage (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) to read and load dictionary files.

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties. However, we may share anonymous information with our partners and service providers to improve the app and provide additional features.

5. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect the collected information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Specifically, we use modern security methods such as encryption and authentication.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with the date of the latest update. We recommend periodically checking this page for changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at Tolkien2007@yandex.ru.

Thank you for using MemoCards!
